# Boards

```base
filters:
  and:
    - file.inFolder("Index & Bases/Index")
    - '!file.tags.contains("remove")'
    - not:
        - file.tags.contains("board")
views:
  - type: cards
    name: View
    order:
      - file.name
    sort:
      - property: file.mtime
        direction: DESC
    cardSize: 120
    imageAspectRatio: 0.85
    image: note.banner

```
